ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE ▶

Founded in 2017, Fore Titans Sports and Events Company has grown into a trusted name in the sports industry, delivering world-class experiences across all levels of the game. From grassroots tournaments to international events, we provide complete solutions in player management, sponsorships, event management, broadcasting, and sports tour management.

With a strong track record of successfully organizing 100+ cricket events in partnership with multiple leading brands, Fore Titans has consistently showcased excellence, professionalism, and innovation. Our goal is to create impactful sporting platforms that nurture talent, engage fans, and connect brands with the passion of sports.

At Fore Titans, we believe sports are more than just competition – they are about opportunity, growth, and inspiration. Whether managing players, executing large-scale events, or building brand partnerships, we remain committed to driving sports to the next level.

Fore Titans – Powering Sports, Players, and Events.

MISSION VISION ▶

MISSION

To nurture talent, create opportunities, and deliver impactful sporting experiences from grassroots to international levels through professional management, strong partnerships, and world-class events.

VISION

To be a global leader in sports and event management, empowering athletes, brands, and fans through innovation, excellence, and passion for sports.

FOUNDERS MESSAGE ▶

As founders, we believe sports are more than just games – they are a force that inspires, unites, and empowers people. Our mission is to bridge the gap between grassroots and global arenas, ensuring that every player, partner, and fan experiences the very best.

We are committed to building Fore Titans into a trusted name in sports, where professionalism, innovation, and integrity drive everything we do. Together, let’s shape the future of sports and create moments that last a lifetime.

Looking ahead, we aim to integrate technology into sports to enhance accuracy, performance, and overall development of the game. From data-driven player analysis to advanced event management systems, we see technology as a game-changing force in the sports industry.

By combining passion with innovation, Fore Titans is not only managing sports but also transforming the way the game is played, experienced, and celebrated.

GAURAV PATIL

DIRECTOR & CEO
